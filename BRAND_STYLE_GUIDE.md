# Rani Beauty Clinic — Brand Style Guide (One Pager)

## 1) Brand Essence & Voice
- **Essence**: A scientific ethereal medspa that elevates your inner beauty to a heavenly glow.
- **Tagline**: “Award Winning Medical Aesthetics”
- **Personality**: Sophisticated, Nurturing, Empowering
- **Tone**: Inspirational, benefit-focused, warm.
  - **Signature phrases**: “Elevate Radiance”, “Unlock Aura”
  - **Messaging focus**: Transformation, confidence, results-with-care.

## 2) Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|---|---:|---|
| **Primary Navy** | `#0F1D2C` | Headlines, backgrounds, key text |
| **Accent Gold** | `#F3D6BE` | Highlights, calls to action, badges |
| **Light Background** | `#FAF8F5` | Page background, sections (airy, premium feel) |
| **Pure White** | `#FFFFFF` | Text on dark backgrounds, negative space |
| **Text Dark** | `#2A2A2A` | Body copy on light backgrounds |

- **Pro tip**: Pair **Navy + Gold** for rich, elevated contrast. Use **Light Background** to keep layouts uncluttered and refined.

## 3) Typography
- **Primary font**: **Montserrat** (weights **400, 600, 700**)  
  - **Usage**: Body text, subheadings, button labels  
  - **Fallbacks**: Arial, Helvetica
- **Secondary font**: **Playfair Display** (Regular, Bold)  
  - **Usage**: Hero headlines, major section titles (editorial feel)  
  - **Fallback**: Georgia

- **Tip**: Use **Playfair Display** for “hero” moments; balance with **Montserrat** for modern clarity and readability.

## 4) Iconography & Imagery

### Angel Wings & Halo
- **Meaning**: Divine transformation; ethereal, uplifting vibe.
- **Usage**: Subtle, low-opacity motifs behind key content or headings.

### Lotus Flower
- **Meaning**: Rebirth, purity, renewal (aligned to medspa experience).
- **Usage**: Primary emblem or accent icon (social, signage, membership materials).
- **Recommended style**: Simplified line art or minimalist silhouette (modern-luxe).

### Photography
- **Style**: High-key, softly lit imagery with confident individuals and glowing skin.
- **Backgrounds**: Neutral / airy to complement the palette.

## 5) Signature Look & Feel
- **Layout & spacing**: Generous margins and spacing; **1.5–2x line height** for an upscale, spa-like ambiance.
- **Rounded corners**: ~**15px** on cards/boxes for soothing modern softness.
- **Shadows & glows**:
  - **Drop shadow**: `box-shadow: 0 10px 30px rgba(0,0,0,0.06)`
  - **Glows**: Subtle radial glows for halos / lotus highlights (keep refined, not loud).

## 6) CTA & Button Styles

### Primary CTA (Default)
- **Background**: Primary Navy `#0F1D2C`
- **Text**: Pure White `#FFFFFF`
- **Shape**: Rounded pill (`border-radius: ~30px`)
- **Type**: Montserrat **600/700**
- **Hover**: Slight lift (`transform: translateY(-3px)`) + soft shadow

### Secondary CTA (Use Sparingly)
- **Background**: Accent Gold `#F3D6BE`
- **Text**: Primary Navy `#0F1D2C`
- **Use for**: Membership sign-ups, promotions, special offers (to spotlight key moments)
